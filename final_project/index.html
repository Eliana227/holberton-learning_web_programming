Common Ground High School

Common Ground High School is an agriculure school that's goal is to connect students to the enviornment. The school is located near a forest allowing students hike, learn more about the outdoors, and get a hands on learning experience. Common Ground is rich in diversity so that many different perspectives and ideas are contributed to group disscusions. Social justice and enviormental justice are also key values to Commom Ground. Common Ground doors are open to all ages not just high school students.
