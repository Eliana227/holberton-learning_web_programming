Saving Money

Everyone likes to get nice items like clothes, accessories and more. Although may not like going broke because of it. For this reason I have created this page to give you some tips on how to save money, and even make some quick money as well.

Honey Groupon Ebay

Honey is a free browser extension that searches for the best coupons/discounts when you are shopping online.

Groupon is an app and website that helps you find discounts, about anything from spas and hotels to painting classes and a trampoline park.

Ebay is an app and a website used to sell any items (that are in good condition) you no longer use.

Setting aside some money that you have earned can also help you save. Even saving a dollar a day can eventually add up.

If you have any items that are in good conditions and no longer use you can consider selling them. You can go the traditional route, and have a garage sale or sell online.

Another way to save money is to have a budget. Which is easier said than done. Although it can help you manage your money responsibly.
