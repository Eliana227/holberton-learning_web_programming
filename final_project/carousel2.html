Ways to deal with stress.

Physical Activites

A healthy way to relieve stress is to engage in physical activites. This includes playing sports like basketball, volleyball, soccer, swimming and more. Taking a walk, riding bikes and hiking outdoors is also very stress relieving.

Relax

Just realxing is also a way to deal with stress. Giving yourself time to calm down can be very benefical. Ways to relax include listening to music, mediation, drawing, playing videogames, and taking a nap to recharge. Writing out you feelings is yet another way to relieve stress.
