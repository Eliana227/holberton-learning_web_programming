Ways to deal with stress.

Physical Activites

A healthy way to relieve stress is to engage in physical activites. This includes playing sports like basketball, volleyball, soccer, swimming and more. Taking a walk, riding bikes and hiking outdoors is also very stress relieving.

Relax

Just realxing is also a way to deal with stress. Giving yourself time to calm down can be very benefical. Ways to relax include listening to music, meditation, drawing, playing videogames, and taking a nap to recharge. Writing out you feelings is yet another way to relieve stress.

Time Mangement Techniques

Sometimes stress is causing by many procrastinating, and doing things last minute (which has happened to me many times). Keep a planner, putting reminders on you phone, and making list are all ways to manage you time efficiently.
